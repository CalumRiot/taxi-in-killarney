Discover Killarney

Our Services:

Taxi Service

We offer a basic taxi service in and around the Killarney area operating on a Wednesday-Sunday 16:00pm-03:00am timeframe. Bookings are accepted outside this timeframe but should be booked 24 hours in advance.

Airport Transfers

We offer airport transfers to Killarney and the surrounding area. We service Cork, Shannon and Dublin International Airports and Kerry Regional Airport.

Guided Tours

Looking to see all the sights that Killarney has to offer? We provide a range of guided tours to some of the best local hotspots such as Slea Head Drive, The Cliffs of Moher & The Ring of Kerry.

Golf Chaffeur

Are you looking for the best links courses Ireland has to offer? Maybe you'd prefer a parklands course? Whatever the preference we can provide. We have years of experience both chaffeuring too aswell as playing on Ireland's Top Golf Courses.
