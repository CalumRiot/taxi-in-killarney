Slea Head Drive Tour

Slea Head is located in the southwest of Ireland along the Dingle Peinsula it has been cited by National Geographic as "the most beautiful place on earth" with Dingle itself being voted among the top one hundred destinations in the world by TripAdvisor. During this tour you can see all the beautiful sites that Dingle Slea Head has to offer such as the Fahan Group Beehive Huts medieval houses dating back over 1000 years, the Stars Wars set location which featured in the 2017 Star Wars Film "Star Wars: The Last Jedi" and of course Slea Head Drive with it's breathtaking views of Dingle Bay and The Blasket Island's.

Ring of Kerry Tour

The Ring of Kerry is a 180km route which takes in parts of the Wild Atlantic Way and passes through many rural Irish villages, towns, countryside and of course Killarney National Park. There are many sites to be seen throughout this tour with Torc Waterfall, Killarney National Park, the Lakes of Killarney, Macgillycuddy's Reeks mountain range as well as beautiful seaside locations such as Cahersiveen, Valentia Island, Rossbeigh Beach and Charlie Chaplin's favorite summer getaway Waterville.

Cliffs of Moher Tour

The Cliffs of Moher are sea cliffs located in the southwestern edge of the Burren in County Clare. They extend for almost 14km and rise to 214 metres at their highest peak. The cliffs are also home to thirty thousand seabirds who make their homes along the face of the cliffs. Your tour will take you up to clare via Tarbert's Shannon Car Ferry which features stunning views of the mouth of the River Shannon. You will pass through delightful rural towns and villages along the coast of County Clare such as Lahinch, Miltown Malbay and Liscannor. You will see Ireland's finest Geopark with it's stunning rock formations, megalithic tombs and the impressive Aillwee Cave with its 1km passage and Bird of Prey Center.

From €340pp

Lakes of Killarney Tour

The Lakes of Killarney are all located within Killarney National Park. They consist of three lakes Lough Leane, Muckross Lake and Upper Lake. This tour will show you all the sites that the Lakes of Killarney have to offer. These include Ross Castle a 15th century Castle built by the Earls of Kenmare it was the last stronghold in Munster to hold out against Cromwell eventually falling into British hands in 1652. Torc Waterfall a 20 metre high waterfall which lies at the base of Torc Mountain and flows into Muckross Lake. Ladies View a well known viewing point over looking the Lakes of Killarney and the surrounding mountain range, it gained it's name from Queen Victoria's ladies-in-waiting they visited the site during Victoria's visit to Ireland in 1861.

From €80pp
